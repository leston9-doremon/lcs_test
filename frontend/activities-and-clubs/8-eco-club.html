Eco Club

The Eco Club at Loretto Central School champions environmental awareness and sustainable living. Students are inspired to be guardians of the planet through hands-on activities, green campaigns, and a deep connection with nature on our beautiful campus.

Meets
Every Wednesday, Lunch Break
Open To
All Classes
Venue
School Garden / Ground

Club Activities

  • Campus plantation drives and garden maintenance
  • Waste segregation and composting projects
  • Rainwater harvesting awareness campaigns
  • Nature walks and biodiversity documentation
  • World Environment Day and Earth Day celebrations
  • No-plastic and reduce-reuse-recycle campaigns

Achievements

  • Maintained and expanded school garden
  • Organised inter-class eco quiz and poster competitions
  • Awareness rallies on environmental conservation
  • Student-led initiatives on reducing single-use plastics
  • Tree plantation drives on special occasions
