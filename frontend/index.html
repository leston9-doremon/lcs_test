About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence & Values

Loretto Central School has been a beacon of quality education for over three decades. Founded on the principles of academic rigour, character building, and holistic development, we are proud to serve thousands of families.

Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi, our school offers a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to Class XII, delivered by a team of highly qualified and passionate educators committed to bringing out the best in every child.
