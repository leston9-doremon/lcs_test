The Loretto Central School, a leading Educational Institution in the vicinity of Loretto Padavu and Bantwal. The natural aura of the beautiful serene environment blended with its passion to provide the best for the students to learn and explore has truly created a powerful atmosphere for every child to learn and excel.

The school is under the management of the Catholic Board of Education with Most Rev. Dr. Peter Paul Saldanha as the Bishop, Rev. Fr. Antony Shera as the Secretary, and Rev. Fr. Francis Crasta as the Manager of the school.

In the year 2010, Loreto English Medium Higher Primary School with 6th and 7th standard comprising of 21 students came into existence. On the very next year the High School section was begun and 58 students enrolled for Class 8th. During the same time the new building construction was taken up with the help of donors. The spacious class rooms and basic facilities were provided.

As time passed, additions were made and the school has now come to a position of completing the extended building. At present, the Correspondent of the School, Rev. Fr. Francis Crasta, has taken up the challenge of completing the extended building work that was stopped during the Covid period.

This year the LKG and UKG sections are newly started and students are happy and peaceful in the campus. The dedicated teachers are always willing to take up the challenge and to impart knowledge.

The school has received the CBSE affiliation in the month of May, up to the secondary level — that is, from LKG to Xth standard. The school is marching ahead under the leadership of Rev. Fr. Jason Monis as the Principal.
